ACCA Manual D Table 3-1 velocities. Rigid supply trunk: 900 FPM max. Rigid return trunk: 700 FPM max. Flex: 700 FPM max. FR max 0.08 IWC/100ft.
Filter face velocity must not exceed 500 FPM per ASHRAE/ACCA guidelines. Velocities above 500 FPM at the filter grille cause excessive pressure drop, noise, and reduced filter life. All MERV ratings are tested per ASHRAE 52.2 at standard face velocities (typically 492 FPM). Actual installed face velocity depends on filter grille size and system CFM.
